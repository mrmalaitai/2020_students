Intellectual Property is when the works belong to the creator and who has the right to use it. 
I hand drew the player, enemy, objects so that I had no Intellectual Property problems

The End user is the user who will be using the product. The product should cater to the specific needs of that user.
People who might like this game are gamers who have a good sense of direction for controls. I made sure it was easy to use by only allowing 4 keys.

Functionality simply looks at whether the product functions as intended and to ensure there are no problems, bugs, crashes etc.
The game loads quickly and there are no bugs or issues.
